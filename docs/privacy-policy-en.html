Data controller

This site is operated by Rise Up Together. For any data-related question, contact hello@riseuptogether.co.uk.

What we collect and why

  • Public forms (volunteer, partners, team registration): name, email, optional phone, and message. We use these to reply to you and coordinate the association's activities.
  • Newsletter: email address and locale (ro/en). We send a confirmation email first; only after confirmation do we send newsletters. You can unsubscribe any time via the link in every email.
  • Cookies: we use a minimal cookie to remember your language preference and the consent banner.

Who we share data with

We rely on the following GDPR-compliant processors:

  • Railway — hosting infrastructure and database.
  • Resend — delivery of transactional and newsletter emails.
  • hCaptcha — bot protection on forms.

We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties.

How long we keep data

  • Form submissions (volunteer, partners, registrations) are kept while relevant to the organization's activity.
  • Newsletter subscribers are kept until they unsubscribe.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you can request access, rectification, deletion or portability of your data. Email hello@riseuptogether.co.uk and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes

We will update this policy whenever processors or purposes change. The current version is the one displayed on this page.
